Who is enterprise IT for? +

Mid-to-large businesses with 50+ employees, significant IT infrastructure, and complex operational technology needs. If you're a smaller business, our Software Development or Automation services may be a better fit.

Do you work with businesses in USA? +

Yes — we have specific experience with IT compliance requirements in USA and the challenges of building enterprise infrastructure in emerging markets.
